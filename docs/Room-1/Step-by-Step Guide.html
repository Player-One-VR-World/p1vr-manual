Step-by-Step Guide

Turn on the number of headsets required (this can be done prior to booking time). Hold the power button for ~5 seconds until the white LED flashes on the front of the headset.

warning

Ensure the headset lenses are wiped prior to handing a customer a headset!

info

Turn on an extra headset and gun controller, as it will be used in case a replacement is needed.

Once each headset is turned on, power the accompanying gun.

Adjust the volume button (the one closer to the nose), by hitting it ~12 times to ensure the headset is at max volume. You can put on the headset to ensure the volume is maxxed out.

warning

It is important to spot check one or two headsets before a group plays, as having max volume drastically increases the quality of the experience.

The ZeroLatency portal should be open & bookmarked on the internet browser. Loading the portal will bring you to the Dashboard. Add Bookings with the + Sign:

Create a booking, following the steps outline below with the labelled image:

  1. Input the purchaser's email, just one of the emails from the group will do
  2. Select the respective game
  3. Input the number of players
  4. Check the "Bypass open time" box
  5. N/A
  6. Input the time 10 minutes in advance, in order to allow time for the briefing and any questions
  7. Hit save

Next, click on Sessions:

Click on Session > select the next upcoming booking session. Click View

Note that when bookings come in, the data must be harvested from the Adult/Child waivers to create the booking session by hand in the zero latency portal. These are google sheets that are accessible from the bookmarks bar:

Following that, on the zero latency session view, input the following data in order:

  1. Email
  2. Display Name
  3. First Name
  4. Last Name
  5. Height (input 2 meters)
  6. Indicate the checkbox for left hand if they said so on the waiver After that repeat steps 1-6 for every remaining player.
info

If there are repeat emails used, such as one for both a child and adult, just put a spam email such as spam123@fakeemailsite.com

  1. Hit save once all the players are inputted.

On the top right of the Zero Latency application, hover over Settings and click Session.

From there select the session you just created according to the time slot and number of players.

Then, on the bottom left of the application, select the Change button and choose the game the group will be playing.

Then, starting from the lowest number headset you powered on, hover over the profile and click Select Profile

Select the first profile from the menu. Repeat this for the 2nd, 3rd, etc. headset and select the corresponding 2nd, 3rd, etc. profile.

On the top right of the application, hover over Settings and click Hardware.

From there, ensure that there are enough headsets active to run the game with enough players, and there must also be a headset on standby. In order to put a headset on standby, click the headset and click the Standby button.

info

To take headsets out of the game you can click Settings > Hardware and from there you can either activate headsets for use in games or put them into standby mode. You can also restart headsets, etc for troubleshooting purposes.

For each player, choose a team color. If they are a group of less than 4 players, you can put them on the same team. Otherwise try to split into two in the best way possible.

info

Teams don't make them face against eachother, its purely for best utilization of the game space. They will be together for battles.

Once they are equipped with their headset, click the Detect Height button under each profile to optimize their in-game height.

If everything is ready to go and there are no issues, you can start the game by hitting the Play triangle button on the bottom middle of the application.
